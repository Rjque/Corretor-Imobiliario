I created this program to help a friend, a real estate agent, post his houses on Facebook.

	What you can do with it:

* Distribute and organize the houses that will be posted daily. 
Facebook detects and blocks any automation, posting will still be manual, 
but with the help of an assistant.

* Share your files with other agents through Google Drive. 
Houses that have already been sold or are added will be detected in the process, 
which means that when you make changes in Drive, they will be changed on your PC.


	To use it, your files need to follow this pattern:

* Keep the photos of your houses taken in separate folders 
(a folder with the title of the house for each house).

* Inside each folder, add a .txt with the title and description of the house 
(your market used in the description of the post, with the price, area of ​​the land, etc.).
